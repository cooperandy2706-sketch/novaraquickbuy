=========================================
  NOVARA QUICKBUY — WINDOWS INSTALL GUIDE
=========================================

STEP 1: Run the installer (Novara-Quickbuy-Windows.exe)

STEP 2: If Windows SmartScreen appears saying
        "Windows protected your PC":

  → Click "More info"
  → Click "Run anyway"

  ⚠️  This warning appears because the app is not yet signed with
      a paid Microsoft certificate. The app is safe to install.

STEP 3: Follow the installer steps and click "Install".

STEP 4: Launch "Novara Quickbuy" from your Desktop or Start Menu.
